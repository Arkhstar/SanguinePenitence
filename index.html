About

Sanguine Penitence is a dark fantasy exploration adventure game where you must wander the wilds hunting cursed beasts to sell for coin.

The Realm has Fallen

The remnants of an old village endures deep in the cursed wilds; the final bastion of humanity, saved from oblivion under the watchful gaze of a long-forgotten deity.

Through offering of silver coin, you have the chance to restore purity and cleanse the world of this terrible rot, and as the sole hunter, it falls to you to hunt the monsters of the wilds and pay this noble tithe.

Day and Night

At the beginning of every day, you have the chance to explore your village, speak with fellow survivors, sell your quarry, repair your gear, and make an offering at the altar.

Once you leave town, you wander the wilds, hunting down cursed monsters through a psuedo-rhythmic ATB system. The Sun slowly sets on your expedition, revealing more dangerous and aggresive beasts as Day turns to Night — but stronger creatures merit greater rewards.

Yet, as time passes, the festering curse crawls closer to the village, threatening what remains of its sanctity.
